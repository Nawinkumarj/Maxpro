Company OKRs and ERP Implementation

Setting Objectives and Key Results (OKRs)

Based on our detailed diagnosis of the company, we formulate the main goals of the company, which we call OKRs (Objectives & Key Results).

  • Objectives are the long-term goals that a company wants to achieve.
  • Key Results are quantifiable KPIs/metrics used to track if the objectives are being achieved.

We assess your employee skill set, organizational culture, current systems & processes, and map them to the software to ensure the software provides reports on all the KPIs/metrics needed.

Key Focus Areas:
  • Organization Culture
  • Employee skills & capabilities
  • SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)
  • Required internal controls
  • Required Data for MIS (Management Information Systems)
Example: Manufacturing Business Objectives

A manufacturing business’s objective may be to fulfill orders quickly by improving production efficiency and the order fulfillment process. To achieve this objective, we develop KPIs to track and take action. These KPIs might include:

  • Average Production per day per machine
  • Average time taken on each production stage
  • Average ‘Order to Delivery’ days
  • Average order fulfillment rate (Qty delivered/qty ordered)

If you're looking for a reliable and experienced ERP implementation consultant in Chennai, Bangalore, Bhopal, or Vijayawada, PKC is the right partner. We ensure that the implemented software provides the necessary data to continuously track and report these KPIs accurately and on time.
